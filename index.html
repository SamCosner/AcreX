How it Works

Tokenize

We partner with farmers to convert farmland into digital tokens, each representing a share of ownership.

Invest

Buy tokens to gain fractional ownership in real, income-generating farmland.

Own

Hold, sell, or trade your tokens anytime. Real assets, real returns, full transparency.

Earn

Receive passive income from farm operations and land value appreciation, distributed directly to your wallet.

Marketplace

The AcreX Marketplace connects you with vetted farm opportunities across the U.S., each offering transparent data on location, projected returns, and long-term growth potential. Whether you're diversifying your portfolio or investing for the first time, our platform makes it easy to compare offerings, understand the risks and rewards, and start owning a piece of productive farmland.
